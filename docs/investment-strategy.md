## üîÑ Rebalancing Strategy

### When to Rebalance

**Annual Rebalancing (Recommended):**
- Pick a date (e.g., January 1, your birthday)
- Check if any asset is off by more than 5%
- Sell high performers, buy low performers
- Use new contributions to bring back to target

**Example:**
```
Target: 70% VTI, 20% VXUS, 10% BND
Current: 75% VTI, 18% VXUS, 7% BND

Action: Sell 5% of VTI, buy 2% VXUS and 3% BND
Or: Invest new money entirely in VXUS and BND
```

### Tax Considerations

**In Roth IRA or 401(k):**
- Rebalance freely - no tax consequences
- Sell winners, buy losers without worry

**In Taxable Brokerage:**
- Be careful - selling triggers capital gains tax
- Use new contributions to rebalance
- Only sell if absolutely necessary
- Tax-loss harvest to offset gains

## üö´ What NOT to Do

### ‚ùå Don't Pick Individual Stocks
- 80% of professionals can't beat the index
- You won't either (nobody can consistently)
- Massive risk for minimal potential reward
- Index funds win long-term

### ‚ùå Don't Time the Market
- Nobody can predict crashes
- Missing the 10 best days costs you 50% of returns
- Stay invested through ups and downs
- "Time in market > timing the market"

### ‚ùå Don't Pay High Fees
- 1% fee vs 0.04% fee = $590K difference over 40 years!
- Actively managed funds rarely beat index
- Avoid funds with expense ratios >0.20%
- Every 0.1% in fees costs you ~$100K in retirement

### ‚ùå Don't Panic Sell in Bear Markets
- Market crashes are BUYING opportunities
- Stocks are on sale
- 2008 crash: -57%... then +400% recovery
- 2020 COVID crash: -34%... then +120% recovery
- Those who stayed in won big

### ‚ùå Don't Over-Diversify
- Owning 50 different funds isn't better
- Three-fund portfolio is plenty
- More complexity ‚â† better returns
- Keep it simple!

### ‚ùå Don't Chase Performance
- Last year's winner is often next year's loser
- Crypto, meme stocks, "hot tips" = gambling
- Stick to boring index funds
- Boring = wealthy

## üí° Advanced Strategies

### Tax-Loss Harvesting (Taxable Accounts Only)

**What it is:**
- Sell losing investments to offset capital gains
- Immediately buy similar (but not identical) fund
- Reduce tax bill while staying invested

**Example:**
- VTI is down 10% = $5,000 loss
- Sell VTI, immediately buy SCHB (similar fund)
- Use $5,000 loss to offset other gains
- Saves $1,000+ in taxes (at 20% capital gains rate)

### Roth IRA Conversion Ladder (For Early Retirement)

**What it is:**
- Convert Traditional 401(k) to Roth IRA
- Pay taxes on conversion in low-income years
- After 5 years, withdraw conversions penalty-free

**When to use:**
- Ages 55-59 before you can access IRA
- You have little/no income (already retired)
- Pay minimal taxes on conversions
- Access money without penalty

### Mega Backdoor Roth (If Available)

**What it is:**
- Contribute after-tax money to 401(k)
- Immediately convert to Roth
- Way to get more than $7K into Roth annually

**Requirements:**
- Employer 401(k) allows after-tax contributions
- Employer allows in-service conversions
- Rare but incredibly valuable if available
