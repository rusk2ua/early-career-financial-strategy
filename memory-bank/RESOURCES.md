## Recommended Brokerage Platforms

### Vanguard
**Website:** https://investor.vanguard.com

**Best For:** Index fund investors, buy-and-hold strategy

**Pros:**
- Created the index fund (founder: John Bogle)
- Lowest expense ratios on index funds
- Client-owned structure (profits returned to investors)
- Excellent for long-term retirement investing

**Cons:**
- Website/app interface is dated
- Limited research tools
- Not ideal for active trading

**Recommended Funds:**
- VTSAX (Total Stock Market Index Fund Admiral Shares)
- VTI (Total Stock Market ETF)
- VTIAX (Total International Stock Index Fund Admiral Shares)
- VXUS (Total International Stock ETF)
- VBTLX (Total Bond Market Index Fund Admiral Shares)
- BND (Total Bond Market ETF)

**Minimum Investments:**
- Admiral Shares: $3,000
- ETFs: 1 share (typically $50-200)

---

### Fidelity
**Website:** https://www.fidelity.com

**Best For:** All-around investing, excellent customer service

**Pros:**
- Zero expense ratio index funds available
- Excellent customer service (24/7 phone support)
- Modern website and mobile app
- Great research tools and educational resources
- No minimum for most funds

**Cons:**
- Slightly higher fees than Vanguard on some funds
- More product options can be overwhelming

**Recommended Funds:**
- FSKAX (Total Stock Market Index Fund) - 0.015% ER
- FTIHX (Total International Index Fund) - 0.06% ER
- FXNAX (US Bond Index Fund) - 0.025% ER

**Special Features:**
- Fidelity ZERO funds (0% expense ratio)
- Fractional share investing
- Excellent IRA tools

---

### Charles Schwab
**Website:** https://www.schwab.com

**Best For:** Active traders, excellent checking account integration

**Pros:**
- No foreign transaction fees on debit card
- Excellent checking account with no fees
- Good for travelers
- Competitive index fund options
- Strong customer service

**Cons:**
- Slightly higher expense ratios than Vanguard
- Interface can be complex for beginners

**Recommended Funds:**
- SWTSX (Total Stock Market Index) - 0.03% ER
- SWISX (International Index) - 0.06% ER
- SWAGX (US Aggregate Bond Index) - 0.04% ER

**Special Features:**
- Bank + brokerage integration
- ATM fee reimbursement worldwide

## Educational Resources

### Books

#### "The Simple Path to Wealth" by JL Collins
**Why Read:** Best beginner book for index fund investing and FI

**Key Concepts:**
- Stock market basics explained simply
- F-You Money concept
- Simple portfolio construction
- Debt avoidance
- Building wealth series (blog-based)

**Target Audience:** Complete beginners to intermediate

---

#### "Your Money or Your Life" by Vicki Robin
**Why Read:** Philosophical foundation for FIRE movement

**Key Concepts:**
- Calculate your real hourly wage
- Track every penny
- Maximize savings rate
- Redefine relationship with money

**Target Audience:** Anyone seeking financial independence

---

#### "The Bogleheads' Guide to Investing" by Taylor Larimore
**Why Read:** Comprehensive guide to passive index investing

**Key Concepts:**
- Asset allocation
- Tax-efficient investing
- Rebalancing strategies
- Estate planning basics

**Target Audience:** Intermediate investors

---

#### "Early Retirement Extreme" by Jacob Lund Fisker
**Why Read:** Extreme frugality and systems thinking

**Key Concepts:**
- 75%+ savings rate strategies
- Self-sufficiency
- Renaissance man approach

**Target Audience:** Those considering very aggressive FIRE

**Warning:** Strategies are extreme; adapt to your situation

## Healthcare Resources

### Healthcare.gov
**Website:** https://www.healthcare.gov/

**Purpose:** ACA marketplace enrollment

**Best For:**
- Healthcare during early retirement (pre-65)
- Subsidy qualification
- Plan comparison

**Key Info:**
- Open enrollment: Nov 1 - Jan 15
- Special enrollment for certain life events
- Income-based subsidies available

---

### Medicare.gov
**Website:** https://www.medicare.gov/

**Best For:** Planning for age 65+ healthcare

**Components:**
- Part A: Hospital insurance (usually free)
- Part B: Medical insurance (~$175/month)
- Part D: Prescription coverage
- Medigap: Supplemental insurance

## Professional Services

When to seek professional help:

### Certified Financial Planner (CFP)
**When to hire:**
- Complex tax situation
- Inheritance or windfall
- Major life changes (marriage, divorce)
- Business ownership

**Look for:** Fee-only fiduciary

---

### CPA (Certified Public Accountant)
**When to hire:**
- Self-employment income
- Rental properties
- Stock options/RSUs
- Complex tax optimization

---

### Estate Planning Attorney
**When to hire:**
- Creating will/trust
- Children estate planning
- Business succession
- High net worth ($500K+)
