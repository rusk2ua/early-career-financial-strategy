## Notes & Reminders

### Documentation Standards
- Use consistent formatting across all markdown files
- Include "Last Updated" footer on all documents
- Cross-reference related documents with links
- Keep examples realistic and specific
- Maintain conversational but technical tone

### Financial Assumptions to Monitor
- 401(k) limits (typically increase $500-$1000/year)
- Roth IRA limits (increase every few years)
- Income phase-out thresholds
- Standard deduction amounts
- Tax brackets (adjusted for inflation)
- Medicare/ACA costs

### Content Quality Guidelines
- Prioritize actionable advice over theory
- Include specific numbers, not just percentages
- Explain the "why" behind recommendations
- Provide alternatives and scenarios
- Keep beginner-friendly while being thorough
